Educational Websites to Remember

  • Khan Academy - Free online courses, lessons, and practice in many subjects.
  • Coursera - Online courses from top universities and companies.
  • edX - Free online courses from universities worldwide.
  • Codecademy - Interactive coding tutorials and courses.
  • Udemy - Online courses on a wide range of topics.
  • freeCodeCamp - Learn to code for free with projects and certifications.
  • Wikipedia - Free online encyclopedia with vast educational content.
  • OpenLearn - Free learning from The Open University.
  • Academic Earth - Free video courses from leading universities.
  • TED - Inspirational talks and educational videos.
  • National Geographic - Educational content about science, geography, and history.
  • ScienceDaily - Latest research news in science, health, and technology.

Educational YouTube Channels

  • CrashCourse - Educational videos on a wide range of subjects.
  • Veritasium - Science and engineering videos.
  • Numberphile - Videos about interesting math topics.
  • CGP Grey - Educational videos on politics, geography, and culture.
